Random Number Guessing Game

Instructions: Try to pick the correct number, within 1-100! After you press start you can guess a number in the input box and press on the guess button to submit an answer. Be fast! You only have a certain amount of time per guess. Every time you guess or run out of time, you use up a chance. if run out of chances before finding the right number you lose

Hint: think about what the number CAN'T be after you make a guess, there will be an indicator to tell if your guess was higher or lower than the actual answer.
